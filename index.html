    Frequently Asked Questions

    Answers to some common questions about our IT services and solutions

    How long does it take to build a website?

    It depends on the complexity of the project. A basic website can take 1-2 weeks, while custom or e-commerce websites may take 4-6 weeks.

    Do you provide ongoing support after project delivery?

    Yes! We offer free basic support for the first month after delivery and also provide affordable maintenance packages for long-term support.

    Can you redesign or upgrade my existing website?

    Absolutely. We specialize in upgrading outdated websites with modern, responsive, and SEO-friendly designs without losing existing content.

    Do you also offer digital marketing services?

    Yes, we provide SEO, social media marketing, and paid ads management to help your business grow online and reach your target audience.

    What technologies do you use for development?

    We work with the latest technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, PHP, WordPress, and more to deliver scalable solutions.

    Is the website fully responsive and mobile-friendly?

    Yes! All our websites are responsive, optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops to ensure the best user experience.
